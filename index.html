About

Strategic thinking meets clean execution.

I'm Nino Bornhäußer, a Berlin-based freelance web designer & developer. I work at the intersection of design and technology — building digital products that don't just look good, but actually work.

My toolkit: WordPress for content-driven sites, Python for backend logic and automation, and HTML/CSS/JS for everything that needs to feel fast and intentional. I'm available on Upwork and Malt.
